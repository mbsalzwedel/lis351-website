The Ultimate College Living Room Makeover

The living room is the heart of any college house. It is where roommates gather, friends visit, and memories are made. Our featured makeover guide walks you through choosing a cohesive color palette, arranging furniture for conversation, incorporating thrifted statement pieces, and adding lighting that makes the space feel warm and inviting.

With just a few intentional design choices, even a basic rental living room can feel personalized and elevated.

Key Makeover Steps

  • Choose a cohesive color palette
  • Layer rugs and textiles for warmth
  • Add plants for a natural touch
  • Use floor and table lamps for softer lighting
